Summary

Monster Hunter Generations runs with no major graphical or audio issues. The game is fully playable from start to finish. Some artifacts may occur in character select screen.

Like many other Monster Hunter games on 3DS, the game has performance issue due to stereoscopic 3D rendering. However, you can turn off the stereoscopic 3D rendering in GAME menu to get much better performance and get rid of the stereoscopic 3D ghosting. If stereoscopic 3D is turned off, the game should run at better performance.

Sometimes small audio glitches can be found but they don’t interfere in gameplay. You may experience slow downs with roars, flash bombs and specific monsters depending on your hardware and the resolution you run the game.

Known issue: Bone Armor chest and Waist pieces cause audio issues in game.
