Summary

Radiant Historia: Perfect Chronology seems to run without issue when played on Citra. On modest hardware, the game sometimes achieves full but mostly runs at a moderate speed. Some effects performed by the game may cause more drastic slowdowns (“prediction” scenes, overworld weather), but otherwise shows no major errors in its graphics, audio or general gameplay.
